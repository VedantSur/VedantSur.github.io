My Story

Welcome! My name is Vedant Surjan.

Ever since my family got their first smartphone, I've been fascinated by how it worked and its ability to do so much in such a small form factor. This curiosity led me to explore and experiment with any tech gadgets that found its way into my hands. I continued down this path of wanting to learn everything about everything, and asking the never-ending "why" questions, so much that I was awarded "The Most Curious Student" in school.

Growing up in arguably the most technologically advanced city in the world (Tokyo), I channeled my interests into understanding not just how technology works but how it has transformed and improved our lives. This eventually evolved into a passion for the tech industry, specifically focusing on devices and tools that have transformed lives for the better. Today, I am in my final year of a Bachelor of Cybersecurity at the University of Technology Sydney (UTS).

Outisde of my studies and work, I enjoy staying up-to-date on the latest news in the cybersecurity space and exploring emerging technologies. Being passionate about learning, I use my curiosity to expand and build my knowledge about anything - not kidding! When I'm not exploring tech, I enjoy spending quality time with friends and family, casual gaming, playing sports and the occasional adventure!
